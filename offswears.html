Whats an "offensive swear"?

Outlines most swears or curses I consider strictly prohibited or offensive.
This excludes any racial slurs or slurs in general, but that doesn't mean they are okay.

What you CANNOT say:

Bitch, Slut, Dick, Ass, Cock, Cum, Semen, Dipshit, Piece of Shit, Pussy, Fag, Faggot, Cunt, Motherfucker, Penis, Piss, Testicle, Tits, Wanker, Whore, anything about harmful drugs (like meth, LSD, etc.), and anything about sex (Blowjob, rimming, doggystyle, etc.)

Do not attempt to use slightly different variants or acronyms of these words, for example "Bish", "POS", "mf", "ahh" etc.

Here's what you CAN say:

"soft" swears, for example:
frick, crap, shoot, flip, heck, dang, darn, bugger, etc.
other swears like damn, hell, and bastard.
and *occasionally* you can say "fuck" and "shit"
(you *will* be timed out or even kicked for swearing too often)

This is also probably not exhaustive, but I'm sure you get the point. you can sometimes say "what the fuck" and "holy shit". but you absolutely cannot call someone a bitch.
